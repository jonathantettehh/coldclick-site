FAQs

What does "qualified" actually mean?
During onboarding, we agree with you on what qualified looks like for your business. It becomes part of our ICP research and our booking process, so we only count meetings that match your criteria.
How does the pricing work?
Pricing is performance-based, so you only pay per qualified call that shows up and is accepted into your AE's pipeline. We'll walk through the exact figures on the discovery call.
What's the commitment?
We work in 90-day campaigns. The first two weeks are setup (infrastructure, messaging, research). The remaining time is live execution where we book qualified calls and refine the messaging based on real market feedback. After 90 days, you can run another campaign with sharper messaging and stronger results, move to a longer engagement, or take everything we've built and run it yourself.
How quickly will we see qualified calls?
After the two-week setup, bookings usually start within the first month. The early period is where we learn what's resonating in your market, refine the messaging, and identify the strongest channels. By the second half of the campaign, the system has real data behind it and the qualified calls compound. If you continue beyond 90 days, that compounding is what makes the next campaign outperform the first.
How is this different from hiring an SDR team?
A fully loaded SDR costs $98K-$173K per year (salary, tools, overhead, commissions) before they book a single meeting. With Coldclick, you pay per qualified call that shows up and is accepted into your AE's pipeline. The deeper difference is structural: SDRs are paid to book meetings. We're paid on the same metric you're measured on, revenue.
Will this work for our industry?
We focus on B2B companies selling to other businesses in the US market. If you have product-market fit and can articulate the value of what you sell to a senior buyer, the system works. We're happy to confirm fit for your specific ICP on the discovery call.
